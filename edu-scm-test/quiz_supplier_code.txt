# Supplier Code of Conduct Quiz

## Question 1: What is the primary goal of the Supplier Code of Conduct?
A) To maximize profits at any cost
B) To ensure ethical behavior and compliance with laws
C) To limit supplier access to information
D) To increase competition among suppliers

Correct Answer: B

## Question 2: Which of the following is NOT a requirement under the Supplier Code of Conduct?
A) Compliance with all legal and regulatory requirements
B) Adherence to sustainable and ethical sourcing
C) Complete disregard for labor rights
D) Commitment to quality and safety standards

Correct Answer: C

## Question 3: How often should suppliers review and acknowledge the Supplier Code of Conduct?
A) Once a year
B) Every five years
C) Upon initial onboarding and then annually
D) Only upon request from the company

Correct Answer: C

## Question 4: What should suppliers do if they become aware of any violations of the Supplier Code of Conduct?

## Question 5: What type of training is recommended for suppliers?
A) Training only relevant to their specific product lines
B) Comprehensive training that covers all aspects of the Supplier Code of Conduct
C) No training is necessary
D) Training only on technical aspects of supply chain management
