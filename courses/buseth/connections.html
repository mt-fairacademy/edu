The Ethical Expedition

Group the 16 terms into 4 categories of 4. Each group shares a common thread from the course. You have 4 attempts.

How to play
Select 4 tiles that belong to the same category, then press Submit. A correct guess reveals the category. The difficulty increases with color — YELLOW is easiest, PURPLE is hardest.
